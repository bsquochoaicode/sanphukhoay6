GII PHU HC BUNG TRNG

Bung trng l mt tuyn kp, m nhn ng thi 2 vai tr ni tit v ngoi tit.

Bung trng ngoi tit m nhn chc nng to non bo cho qu trnh th tinh.

Bung trng ni tit m nhn chc nng sn xut cc steroid sinh dc chun b cho qu trnh lm t ca trng th tinh v duy tr thai k.

Hnh 1: Gii phu ca t cung v bung trng

Bung trng l mt c quan nm trong bng v khng c thanh mc bao ph.

C hai bung trng, mt bn phi v mt bn tri nm p vo thnh bn ca chu hng, pha sau dy chng rng.

Bung trng c hnh mt ht u dt, nng t 4-8 gam,  kch thc khong 2 cm x 3 cm x 3 cm. Bung trng c mu hng nht, b mt bung trng thng nhn cho ti lc dy th, sau cng ngy cng sn si v hin tng rng trng to thnh nhng vt so trn b mt.

Bung trng c hai mt: mt trong v mt ngoi. Mt trong li, tip xc vi cc tua ca phu vi t cung v cc quai rut. Mt ngoi p vo phc mc ca thnh bn chu hng nm trong h lm gi l h bung trng.

H bung trng c gii hn do cc thnh phn nm ngoi phc mc i ln. Pha trc di l dy chng rng, pha trn l ng mch chu ngoi, pha sau l ng mch chu trong v niu qun. y h l ng mch rn, mch v thn kinh bt. ngi sanh nhiu ln, bung trng c th sa xung thp hn nm trong h Claudius.

Mt ngoi bung trng c mt vt lm gi l rn bung trng l ni mch mu v thn kinh i vo bung trng.

Bung trng c hai b: b t do v b mc treo bung trng. B t do quay ra pha sau v lin h vi cc quai rut, cn b mc treo th c mc treo treo bung trng vo mt sau dy chng rng.

Bung trng c hai u: u vi v u t cung. u vi hng ln trn, l ni bm ca dy chng treo bung trng. u t cung nh hn, quay xung di, hng v pha t cung, l ni bm ca dy chng ring bung trng.

Bung trng c treo l lng trong phc mc nh mt h thng dy chng v mc treo bung trng.

ng mch bung trng tch ra t ng mch ch bng vng tht lng i trong dy chng treo bung trng vo bung trng u vi, ngoi ra cn c nhnh bung trng ca ng mch t cung.

CA 125

CA 125 c lin quan n hot ng ca cc biu m c ngun gc su xa lin quan n phc mc nguyn thy.

CA 125 (hay MUC16) l mt glycoprotein ging mucin. Trong thi k bo thai, CA 125 c ngun gc t thng m phc mc nguyn thy (fetal coelomic epithelia) l ngun gc ca mng lt h Mullerian v biu m s khai b mt sinh dc nguyn thy.  Tng t, ngi trng thnh, CA 125 c ngun gc t cc biu m c ngun gc trung b nh mng bng, mng phi, mng ngoi tim, v cc biu m ca cc c quan ngun gc Mullerian: vi trng, ni mc t cung v c trong c t cung.

Nng tng cao ca CA 125 c lin quan n hot ng ch tit ca cc t bo ung th c lin quan n cc biu m ny.

Khi c ung th thuc cc c quan c lin quan n thng m phc mc nguyn thy, k c cc t bo c ngun gc xa xa c lin quan n phc mc nguyn thy nh ung th bung trng th CA 125 c tng tit v to nng huyt thanh cao bt thng ca CA 125. CA 125 cao cn thy trong cc bnh l c tnh khc nh ung th bung trng, v, phi, ty, i trng v tiu ha khc.

Tuy nhin, nhiu tnh trng khng tn sinh, nhng c kch thch cc biu m ny cng gy nn tnh trng tng ca nng CA 125. CA 125 tng trong mt s trng thi sinh l nh thai k, lc ni mc t cung, khi hnh kinh.  CA 125 cng tng trong mt s bnh l lnh tnh nh trn dch mng bng v/hoc mng phi do lao, suy tim, hi chng thn h

Hin nay, gi tr 35 n v/mL thng c dng l gi tr ngng phn bit (cut-off point) bnh thng vi bt thng. Tuy nhin, i vi nhng ngi mn kinh hoc ct t cung km hai bung trng th nn chn gi tr ngng thp hn. Ngng ct c t 20-26 n v/mL.

Trong cc ung th bung trng c ngun gc biu m nguyn thy,  CA 125 thng tng trong u thanh dch hn l trong u nhy, t bo sng hoc bu gip bin c.

Phn tch gi tr ca CA 125 trong ung th bung trng cho thy vai tr ca CA 125 thay i ty theo giai on ca ung th.

V th CA 125 khng c dng chn on v tm sot s cp cc giai on sm ca ung th bung trng.

Nu c dng cho mc ch tm sot s cp (primary screening) cho ung th bung trng, th CA 125 l mt cng c khng c gi tr. nhy ca CA 125 cho tm sot ung th bung trng khng triu chng ch l 10%, vi c hiu l 30%. nhy ca tm sot ung th bung trng bng CA 125 khng cao hn 50% trong ung th bung trng giai on sm. V th CA 125 khng c dng chn on v tm sot s cp cc giai on sm ca ung th bung trng.

nhy ca tm sot ung th bung trng bng CA 125 c tng ln, t n 85% khi ung th tin trin xa. Tuy nhin, iu ny khng gip nng cao v tr ca CA 125, do khi ung th tin trin xa th cc phng tin hnh nh vn c mt gi tr cao hn CA 125 rt nhiu.

CA 125 l ch bo cho mc ch pht hin sm ti pht ca cc loi ung th bung trng c ngun gc biu m.

Trong theo di sau phu thut cc loi ung th bung trng c ngun gc biu m chung (tr ung th tuyn dch nhy), CA 125 chng t n l mt ch bo hu ch. Trong mc ch pht hin sm ti pht, nhy ca CA 125 ln n 60%. nhy cao l u th ca CA 125 trong theo di, bt chp chuyn bit ca test rt thp trong mc ch ny, ch c 20%.

HE4

HE4 l ch bo cho mc ch pht hin sm ti pht v tin trin ca cc ung th bung trng c ngun gc biu m.

HE4 (Human Epididymal Secretory Protein 4) c nhn bit ti biu m phn xa mo tinh v c cho l cht c ch protease gip tinh trng trng thnh. Tuy nhin chc nng c th ca HE4 vn cha c xc nh.

HE4 tng cao trong 93% u tuyn dch trong, 100% u dng ni mc t cung v 50% u t bo sng ca bung trng. Tri li, HE4 tng t trong u tuyn dch nhy.

Tng t CA 125, HE4 cng thay i theo tui v nhiu tnh trng khc.

nhy ca HE4 cho mc tiu chn on ung th bung trng khong 67%. chuyn bit cho mc tiu chn on ung th bung trng ca HE4 c th ln n trn 90%. Do cc tnh cht ny, FDA cng nhn HE4 l ch bo sinh hc dng theo di s ti pht, pht trin ca cc loi ung th bung trng c ngun gc biu m.

HE4 thng c thc hin chung vi CA 125, vi kt qu din gii nguy c c c nh mt ton gi l ROMA test.

ROMA test

ROMA test l mt ton s dng cng mt lc 2 ch bo khi u l CA 125 [13] v HE4, kt hp vi c im c nhn v tin s bnh nhn.

Nhn chung, ROMA test (Risk of Ovarian Malignancy Algorithm) kt hp kho st HE4 vi CA 125 c   nhy cao hn so vi kho st tng ch bo ring l.

ROMA test cho php phn tng cc bnh nhn c khi u vng chu ra 2 nhm: (1) nhm bnh nhn c nguy c cao v (2) nhm bnh nhn c nguy c thp ca ung th c ngun gc biu m ca bung trng.

C s khc bit v im ct (cut-off) hai dn s khc nhau: (1) nhm trc mn kinh v (2) nhm sau mn kinh.

ROMA test c mt gi tr d bo m (NPV) rt cao, ln n 99%.

94% trong tng s cc ung th biu m bung trng c nhn din vo nhm nguy c cao khi s dng ROMA test.

Trong mc tiu chn on ung th bung trng, HE4 c chuyn bit l 93.6%, v l ch bo c chuyn bit cao nht so vi CA 125 (82.1%) v ROMA (82.4%).

Cng vi mc tiu chn on ung th bung trng, nhng trong nhm ph n tin mn kinh, chuyn bit ca HE4 cho chn on ung th bung trng ln n 93.8%, v vn l ch bo c chuyn bit cao nht so vi CA 125 (76.3%) v ROMA (85.1%).

Tuy nhin, nhm ph n mn kinh, CA 125 v ROMA c kh nng phn bit  bit cao hn so vi HE4.

Lu rng ROMA test khng c dng cho mc ch tm sot ung th biu m bung trng.

NANG HOANG TH (CORPUS LUTEUM CYST)

Hong th l mt cu trc chc nng, c thnh lp sau phng non, do s bin i ca cc t bo v-ht thnh t bo hong th di tc dng ca luteinizing hormone.

Hong th c nhim v ch tit estrogen v progesterone, chun b cho lm t ca trng th tinh v duy tr thai k. Progesterone ca hong th gy mt phn hi m trn h i, lm gim nhp ch tit xung GnRH, dn n gim nhp iu cc xung LH, vi h qu l ly giai t nhin ca hong th.

Bt thng ca hong th c th n t:
